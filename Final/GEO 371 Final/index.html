Satellite Riddles

This web mapping application was designed as a fun educational tool. The questions encourage the user to apply their geographic knowledge to deduce where they are, based solely on satellite imagery. This game does not require you to memorize what a specific mountain, river, or city looks like from above. Rather, you are required to search the environment for context clues. Is the mountain covered in snow? That's a good indication that is located in a high latitude region, which could narrow down your choices. Scroll down to navigate between questions. Good luck!
